Wellington Combined Taxis

Wellington Combined Taxis are leading the way in safe, reliable and convenient taxi services. With a fleet of 500 cars, 9 Vans and 30 Shuttles we are your total transport solution.

When you travel with Wellington Combined Taxis, it is our goal to exceed your expectations in both quality, service and safety. All our drivers undergo extensive training and wear their uniforms with pride.

Travel in one of our late model vehicles that are all equipped with GPS systems, security cameras and braille stickers. Feel comfortable knowing you are in the safest hands.
